Privacy Policy

Tactus - Habit Tracking App

Last Updated: February 9, 2025

Summary: Tactus is designed with privacy first. All your habit tracking data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Tactus stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Habit Data: Habits you create, tracking history, and completion records
  • App Preferences: Your notification settings and app customization choices
  • Subscription Status: Information about your active subscriptions (managed through Apple)

Data We Do NOT Collect

Tactus does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location data
  • Your device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Your browsing history or app usage patterns
  • Any personally identifiable information

2. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous user identifier
  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Device type and OS version

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple Services

Tactus uses Apple's native frameworks for:

  • Local Notifications: Reminder notifications are generated locally on your device
  • Live Activities: Real-time habit tracking widgets on your lock screen (iOS 16.1+)
  • In-App Purchases: Managed through Apple's App Store

Your data processed by Apple services is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Permissions

Tactus requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you habit reminders (optional, you can disable anytime)

We do NOT request access to:

  • Camera or Photos
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • Microphone
  • Health data

4. How We Use Your Data

Since all your habit tracking data stays on your device:

  • Habit Tracking: Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of tracking your habits
  • Notifications: Local notifications are generated on your device based on your preferences
  • Subscription Management: RevenueCat processes subscription data to enable premium features

We do not use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Analytics or tracking
  • Sharing with third parties (except RevenueCat for subscription management)
  • Training AI or machine learning models

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your habit tracking data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Deleting individual habits within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)

Subscription data: RevenueCat retains subscription records as required for payment processing and tax compliance. Contact us at privacy@ddmmedia.co to request deletion of subscription records.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Local data is protected by your device's security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Subscription communication with RevenueCat uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS)
  • We do not store your data on our servers, eliminating server-side security risks

7. Children's Privacy

Tactus is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@ddmmedia.co.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your habit data within the app
  • Delete: Remove habits or uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Control Notifications: Disable notifications in iOS Settings → Tactus → Notifications
  • Cancel Subscriptions: Manage subscriptions in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Tactus after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
